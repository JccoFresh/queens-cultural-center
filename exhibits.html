Exhibits

Veni Vidi Vici Exhibit

The "Veni Vidi Vici" exhibit is meant to showcase the influence Queens culture has had on New York City.

Jewish Exhibit

Learn about the history of the Queens Jewish Community; from its origin to the impact it's culture has on Queens.

Hispanic Art Exhibit

The Hispanic Art Exhibit showcases historical art from our Hispanic community. It showcases the morals of the artists and the suffering of the people.

African American Musicians

Learn about African American Msuicians that were born in Queens. Learn about the triumphs and the impact they had on the music world.
